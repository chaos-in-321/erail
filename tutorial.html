Step 1 - Remove speed sensor cover

  • Remove speed sensor cover

  • 1.Remove 4 outside bolts on the Speed Sensor Protector and Bearing Protector located on the non driving end.
    2. Take Out the Bearing Cover

    Tool used in this procedures are:

    Hex Wrench Size 20mm

Step 2 - Loosen speed sensor disk

  • Loosen Speed Sensor Disk

    Loosen all three side bolts of speed sensor disk
    Note : Leave one bolt in the slot and take out other two

Step 3 - Loosen NDE Fixed Labyrinth

  • Loosen NDE Fixed Labyrinth

  • 1. Loosen 6 bolts of the NDE Fixed Labyrinth
    2. Take out only 4 bolts but leave 2 in place

    Tool used in this procedures are:

    Allen Key Size 15mm
